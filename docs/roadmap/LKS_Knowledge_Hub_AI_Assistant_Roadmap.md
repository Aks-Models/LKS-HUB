# Vision

Build a multilingual professional platform that combines:

- Personal website
- Professional Knowledge Hub
- AI Assistant
- Automated content publishing
- RSS aggregation
- Social media automation

The objective is to establish the LKS website as a trusted source of international business development knowledge while significantly reducing the manual effort required to maintain fresh, relevant content.
