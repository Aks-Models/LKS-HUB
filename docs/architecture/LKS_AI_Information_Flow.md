# LKS AI Information Flow

## Purpose

This document defines the information flow of the LKS HUB intelligent system.

The goal is to explain how external information enters the system, how AI processes it, where the knowledge is stored, and how the processed intelligence is reused by the website assistant, business intelligence reports, and automation workflows.

---

## Core Information Flow

```text
Internet / External Sources
        ↓
RSS Sources
        ↓
RSS Collector
        ↓
AI Analyzer
        ↓
Knowledge Base
        ↓
Business Intelligence
        ↓
Website AI Assistant
        ↓
Automation Engine
